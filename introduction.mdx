## What is OMNI?

Most of your day is scattered across a dozen apps — tasks in one place, notes in another, calendars somewhere else, finances in a spreadsheet you forgot about. **OMNI** brings all of that into the one place you're already staring at: your terminal.

No context switching. No browser tabs. No notifications pulling you away. Just you and a blinking cursor, getting things done.

## Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Built for terminal natives">
    If you live in the terminal, your productivity tools should too. OMNI gives you a fast, distraction-free way to handle the things that matter, right where you already are.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Intelligent autocomplete">
    Start typing any command and OMNI suggests completions in real-time. Press `Tab` to accept or keep typing to filter suggestions.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Beautiful gradient interface">
    OMNI uses cosmic purple-to-cyan gradients and thoughtful color coding to make your terminal experience delightful without being overwhelming.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Extensible architecture">
    The command router and prompt system are built to scale. Add new features and commands without touching core infrastructure.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Philosophy

OMNI isn't trying to replace every app on your phone — it's trying to give you a fast, distraction-free way to handle the things that matter, right where you already are.

The foundation is in place: the shell, the prompt, the command router. What gets built on top is where it gets interesting.
